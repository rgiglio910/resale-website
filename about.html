About the Shop

Home Shop About Where to Buy

This shop began as a way to manage space and simplify life while in college, selling items to declutter and keep things organized. Over time, it grew into a larger resale venture, focused on retro and Y2K pieces, including specialty brands like Juicy Couture.

All listings are photographed, listed, and managed independently across eBay, Depop, and Poshmark. This reflects a commitment to quality, care, and sustainability.

To date, I have sold nearly 1,000 items, with hundreds of positive reviews highlighting both the quality of the products and the attention to customer service in every listing.
